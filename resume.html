Words of Advice

These are some tips I heard that helped me during the application process. Feel free to listen, or not. It's up to you.

1. Try to get fee waivers. All 30 of the schools I applied to were free.

2. Be niche and yourself. No one likes a basic, run of the mill applicant.

3. If you want to get into a top school and only care about its ranking/prestige, consider applying Early Decision.

4. Don't limit yourself to a set amount of schools. Apply to as many as you want. You miss 100% of the shots you don't take.

5. Have fun! Applying to college gets boring and tedious when you aren't obsessed with the process.
